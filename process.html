Our Process

The initial process involves determining exactly what type and how much damage you sustained. Then we review the type of roof you have to formulate a plan to move forward if need be. If this involves an insurance claim, we advise on how and when to file, and what works best in speaking with the insurance claims adjuster. All of these factors make a major difference in your success of getting the carrier to 'honor' his or her side of the policy. Remember, insurance carriers are not in the business to pay claims, but to collect premiums. And that is why you need an advocate such as Sewell Consulting.


If a claim is initiated, we meet with the adjuster or carrier representatives:

  • Show them why they need to pay based on the damage, building or wind codes or condition of the roof.
  • Know how to represent on equal footing, because we are in the business.

Once the claim paperwork comes back:

  • Review to verify that you have been compensated correctly.
  • Contact the carrier if not, and submit the corrections.
  • Utilize the same software they do to make certain your compensation is accurate.

Then we provide you with a list of vetted, insured roofing companies:

  • Specialize in the type of repairs you need.
  • Recommend companies that we have used, who have a proven track record with us.

During the roofing work:

  • Verify you are getting above average products and quality workmanship.
  • Help you file the required paperwork to finalize your claim.
  • Supplement for any additional items that were not apparent before construction began.

Finally, we verify or negotiate the warranties you will be getting, either through the manufacturer or installer.
